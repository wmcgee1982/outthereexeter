Mission


Our mission is to strengthen connections between communities and groups to celebrate LGBTQIA+ culture and work to create an inclusive future for LGBTQIA+ people. Click here for more information.

Purpose


Our purpose is to celebrate, promote and uplift the LGBTQIA+ community across Exeter through arts and culture. We do this by enabling emerging and existing LGBTQIA+ groups and individuals to highlight and showcase their work through an annual arts and culture festival and other means that support at least one of the following:

  • promoting the wellbeing of the Exeter LGBTQIA+ community
  • improving the lives of Exeter LGBTQIA+ peoples
  • enhancing the LGBTQIA+ culture across the Exeter community

Goals


Our aims to enrich Exeter's LGBTQIA+ groups and all branches of the community across the city can be achieved through four tangible goals:

  1. Foster stronger relationships between LGBTQIA+ members and the wider community
  2. Create and promote an LGBTQIA+ directory of groups, partners, mentors, programmes, and opportunities that support LGBTQIA+ people to thrive and connect them with relevant supportive resources and networks.
  3. Host an annual festival in collaboration with LGBTQIA+ organisations and individuals, to profile and raise awareness of the culture, support and opportunities available to the community.
  4. Provide year-round opportunities for LGBTQIA+ groups to build a sense of belonging, have a positive impact on the environment and make Exeter a more welcoming place to live, work and visit.
